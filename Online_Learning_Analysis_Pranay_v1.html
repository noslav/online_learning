The purpose of this questionnaire was to get information from our initial target groups. Namely : Coworking space members and Students about their online learning habits. The reason we took Coworking space groups is because they are quite representative of the three groups, freelances, regular employees, interns / students. So is a good place to start.¶

This questionnaire was designed to understand the patterns of how people learn online. It was completely anonymous and took on an average about 5 minutes to complete.
